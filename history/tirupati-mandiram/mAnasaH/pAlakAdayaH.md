## क्षेत्रपालो रुद्रः
Rudra is the kshetrapalaka of Tirumala. this is authentic. every year, on shiva ratri, the archakas go to a shila 1 km away from the temple, and do rudrabhisekham there to it. 

They have a tradition of touching the keys to the kshetrapalaka shila (of which a piece is there in the temple near the dhvajastambham ) with keys of sanctum both while entering and leaving.
