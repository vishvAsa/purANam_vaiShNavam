## Revenue
Tirumala temple generates more money than the entireity of all temples in south india combined. Just last year (2024), the main hundi ( only one hundi, only cash ) generated was 1543 crores . 

Apart from the hundi, there is sale of prasadam (laddus etc) , and every arjita seva has separate tickets, and then tickets from darshanam , accommodation etc and separate donations , brings the annual revenue to well over 5000 crores.
