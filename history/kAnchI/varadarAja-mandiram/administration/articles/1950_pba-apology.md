D No. 325|1360  
From  
Sri. K. Madhava Row,  
Executive Trustee.  
Sri Devarajaswami Devasthanam, Kancheepuram. 13--11--50.

To  
The Secretary,  
The Hindu Religious Endowments Board,  
Madras.

Sir,

Sub:- High handed action shown by the thergalai people on 10--11--50- disciplinery action proposed to take against four persons report submitted to the Board (Vide D.No.316/1360 dated 11-11-50)

Since the submission of the report to Board dated 11--11--50, Sri P.B. Annangarachariar the leader of the Thennacharya darsana saba ( thengalai community) and the persons who assaulted ane on 10--11--50 came to me and tendered their unconditional appology and also repented their action in the presence of Sri T. Krishnamachariar, retired Inspector of police and some respectable gentlemen and also accepted their misdeeds. 

Hence I have decided to stop further proceedings upon them as proposed in the letter sent to the Board dated 11--11--50. 

I have warned them severely. This may be recorded and the report may be lodged.

[signed]  
Executive trustee.

Copy to  
Sri R. Varada Tatachariar  
Hon. Trustee  
Sri Sudarshana Tatachariar  
Hon. Trustee
