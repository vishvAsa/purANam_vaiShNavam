### En
THE THATHACHARI COMMUNITIES ARE ENJOYING THE TRUSTEESHIP FOR CENTURIES. IN THE PROPOSED MODIFICTION OF SCHEME, TO PUT AN END TO ALL DISPUTES IT CAN BE AMENDED IN ANY OF THE METHODS SUGGESTED:

This is the only one temple where Brahmins are holding trusteeship in HRCE Temples in the whole of Tamilnadu. While other communities which have exclusive trusteeship on community basis are not disturbed, this particular temple is targeted by Department. There are no allegations of corruption or mismanagement. 

The only problem is non availability of Brahmin Executive Officers who are appointed as Executive Trustees as per HC scheme. The Commissioner can simply amend the scheme to the effect any body from the Department can be appointed as Executive Trustee. That will solve the whole issue.

Replacing by a 5 member board with Thenkalai, Vadakalai, Harijan, castehindus, women will open pandoras box.

When there is no specific charges of mismanagement, why the scheme should be widened. A short notes of Trusteeship issue of Sri Varadarajaswamy Temple.

1.	Sri Varadarajaswamy Temple is the third holiest of 108 divyadesams next to Srirangam and Tirupati. For Vadakalai cult of Vaishnavas this is the most important divyadesam.
2.	Sri Lakshmikumara Thathadesikan a great Acharya of 17th century was the Rajaguru of Vijayanagara Kings. He developed this temple along with many temples like Srirangam and Tirupathi.
3.	Kanchipuram Thathachar Families are direct descendents of Sri Lakshmiumara Thathadesigan. He administered the temple during his life time. His service was well recognized by various sects during the relevant period. Subsequent to his life time, the temple was continued to be administered by the family members of his decedents.
4.	In 1941, after a repeated dispute by the Thegalai Sect, The Division Bench of Hon’ble Madras Court, settled the Scheme for the Administration of the Sri Devarajaswami Devasthanam, Kancheepuram. By the said scheme, Two Thathachars and one smartha or madhva Brahmin have to form a trust board and the said Smartha or Madhva Brahmin Truestee has to be appointed by HR&CE board.
5.	Ever since then, the Board is consisted of 3 Brahmins only- Two from Vadakalai Thathachar families and the third from the Department who is a non vaishnav Brahmin.
