Proceedings of the Executive Trustee of
Sri Devarajaswami Devasthanam, Kancheepuram.

L.No. 190/1368 dated 12-11-58.

Read petition dated 11-9-58 by Sri. R. Krishnaswami thathachariar and three others.

---
After considering all the facts relating to the matter and taking into consideration the recent developments in Sri Thoopral Vedantha Desikar Shrine relating to the objection of the Vadahalais to the recitation of "Upadesarathinamalai" by thengalais and the consequent order of the Police restraining the thengalais from the reciting Upadesarathinamalai which is not a common prabandam and in view of the fact that the practice in both these temples are said to be common, and the keen tension prevailing among the vadahalais and thengalais in this issue and as festivals are fast approaching, to avert any untoward incidents in the temple the following directions are issued tentatively after discussion with the parties to ensure peaceful conduct of services in this temple, without prejudice to the rights and contentions of parties if any. 

1.  Thengalais are permitted to recite Upadesarathinamalai     during the Anadhyanam period commencing from after     Thirukarthigai and ending with Anushtanakulam     festival, except in Sri Desikar shrine and Sri     Thathadesikar shrine, on occasions when the usual     Melasira Diviya Prabhandam could not be recited. 
2.  To recite Nalpatram on all Alwar, Achariyal sathumurai     excluding Sri Desikar and Sri Thathadesikar sathumurai,     and to recite in the procession of Sri Perumal from     Thiruvadikoil on Sri Manavala mamunigal sathumurai     festival.
3.  To recite on the 6th day morning (Moolam) of the
    Brahmothsavam during the return procession from
    Thiruvadi koil.

---
The thengalais are restrained from reciting Upadesarathina- malai and other non-common prabandams in the Desikar shrine on all the days in the year including Desikar sathumurai festival, Sri Thathadesikar sathumurai festival and on the Sravanam and Anusham Thirunakshethiram xxx days including Anadhyanam days. 

The parties will do as well to conform to the above directions in their own interest and in the interest of the smooth working of the services in the temple. 

This order will come into force from to-day.

(Sd.) V.K. VISWANATHAN,
Executive Trustee.
