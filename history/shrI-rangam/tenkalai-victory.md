## koil olugu
Tk won Srirangam as their temple by using koil olugu which was being maintained by a tenkalai 'accountant'. The record doesn't mention any vdk contribution to the temple. Only a century later, when inscriptions were being read, people saw that the koil olugu is not a full record of the temple as it was claimed in 1803

## Archaka suppression
> The Archakas, who wear Vadakalai mark upon their foreheads, cannot even dream of introducing any Vadakalai custom into the temple. That is Ramanujarya Divyajna! They cannot prostrate before God, twice in the temple. If they dare to do so they are liable to be punished. - PB aNNangarAryaH Source: [TW](/rAmAnujIyam/paramparA/kalai-quarrel/kAnchy-aNNangarAryaH/dayApAtra-history)

>  Tk sthalatthars were forcing them to do so (prostrate once). Even fined them for prostrating more than once. Arcakas went to court and got order in their favor. - RL
