[श्री देवराज अष्टकम्](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/index.php/2015/12/sri-dhevaraja-ashtakam-telugu/)

[![thyaga-mandapam](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/thyaga-mandapam.jpeg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/thyaga-mandapam.jpeg)

*श्रीमत् कंचि मुनिम् वंदे कमलापति ननधनम् ।  
वरधान्ग्रि सदा सन्ग रसायन परायणम् ॥*

#### [श्रोतव्यं](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-00-thaniyan-1)

श्री कमलपति पुत्रुलु, मरियुनु निरतमु वरदराज श्री चरणमुलनु आश्रयुन्चुकॊनि वुंडि वाटि अमृतमुतो कलिसि ममॆकमैनट्टि [श्री तिरुक्कच्चि नम्बि](https://guruparamparaitelugu.wordpress.com/2014/03/19/thirukkachi-nambi/) स्वामि यॊक्क श्री चरणालकु प्रणाममुलु.



*देवराज दया पात्रम् श्री कंचि पूर्ण म् उत्तमम् ।  
रामानुज मुनिर् मान्यम् वंदे हम् सज्जनाश्रयम् ॥*

#### [श्रोतव्यं](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-00-thaniyan-1)

#### 

श्री वरदराज पॆरुमाळ् यॊक्क कृपकु पात्रुलु, [श्री रामानुजु](https://guruparamparaitelugu.wordpress.com/2013/09/16/emperumanar/)लकु गौरवनियुलै, मंचि मनुषुलकु आश्रितुलैनटु वंटि वारैन [श्री तिरुक्कच्चि नम्बि](https://guruparamparaitelugu.wordpress.com/2014/03/19/thirukkachi-nambi/) स्वामि श्री चरणालकु प्रणाममुलु.

**pAsuram 22**

*inRO thiruvAdippUram emakkAga*  
*anRo ingu ANdAL avathariththAL – kunRAdha*  
*vAzhvAga(na) vaikuntha vAn pOkam thannai igazhndhu*  
*AzhvAr thirumagaLArAi           
* 22

#### [Liten](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/upadhesaraththinamalai-22)

**Word by word meaning**

*thiru AdippUram* – beautiful month of Adi’s pUram star (AsAdha, pUrva)  
*inRO* – is it today!  
*emakkAga anRO* – Isn’t it for us only,  
*vAzhvu Aga* – to have life  
*kunRAdha* – not having any shortcomings (of this material world),  
*igazhndhu* – (that she) left  
*vaikuntham* – SrI vaikuNtam of  
*vAn* – best  
*pOkam thannai* – comforts,  
*ANdAL* – (that is,) [sUdik koduththa nAchiyAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/)  
*avathariththAL* – had incarnated  
*ingu* – in this world  
*AzhvAr thirumagaLArAi* – as the divine daughter of [periyAzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html).

**vyAkyAnam**

Out of the three mentioned in the previous pAsuram, [he (maNavALa mAmunigaL)](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/) starts with talking about [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) who is the best among them, using three pAsurams; in this pAsuram [he](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/) melts by engagement of [his](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/) experience, in the thought whether today is *thiru Adip pUram (AsAdha, pUrva)* when [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) divined her incarnation to help [him](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/) reach the goal.

***inRO thiruvAdippUram*** – Is it today what is famously known as thiruvAdippUram? Is it today that we were granted such a day? If we got what is rare, then, doesn’t that become cause for enjoyment?

[He](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/) is divining the reason next.

***emakkAga anRO ingu ANdAL avathariththAL*** – Like when her baby falls in a well a mother would also jump in the same way to save it, ([she](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) incarnated to) take us out who have drowned in births (in this material world)? [She](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) who is the mother for all beings has divined [her](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) incarnation here. Like said in “*kUpaththil vIzhum kuzhaviyudan kudhiththu avvApaththai nIkkum andha annaiyaip pOl* \[Arththip prabandham – 9\]” (like the mother who also falls into the water and removes the danger), and in “*kUpAntharmukthaputhram pathAthamanupathan mAthruvath”* (Like how a mother would jump in to save the baby that fell into the well), [he](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/) who is completely knowledgeable is considering [her](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) incarnation as for [him](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/).

[![ANDALpainting](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/ANDALpainting.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/ANDALpainting.jpg)*SrI ANdAL*

Next it talks about the rarity of such incarnation, by ‘*kunRAdha’,* etc.

Unlike the experience of other places which are not complete/not without shortcomings, in SrI vaikuNtam there is no hindrance to any greatness where limitless [emperumAn](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) is to be enjoyed – discarding such enjoyment, here too ‘*pOkaththil vazhuvAdha pudhuvaiyar kOn kOthai* \[nAchchiyAr thirumozhi 8.10\]’ (As the daughter of [periyAzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html) who enjoys [emperumAn](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) fully), [she](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) incarnated ‘*AzhvAr thirumagaLArAi* (as [AzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html)’s divine daughter, her highness)’.

Like how a queen would give less importance to the flowery bed of the king and lie down holding her baby’s cradle, [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) too ignored the limitless enjoyment of paramapadham, and came incarnating here.

Isn’t this what creates sorrow in the mind of [maNavALa mAmunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/)?

**pAsuram 23**

*periyAzhvAr peN piLLaiyAy ANdAL piRandha*  
*thiruvAdip pUraththin seermai – oru nALaikku*  
*uNdO manamE uNarndhu pAr ANdALukku*  
*uNdAgil oppu itharkum uNdu* 23

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/upadhesaraththinamalai-23)

**Word by word meaning**

*uNdO* – Is there  
*seermai* – greatness  
*oru nALaikku* – in any other day (compared to)  
*thiru AdippUraththin* – the beautiful Adi month pUram star day  
*ANdAL* – (in which) [sUdik koduththa nAchchiyAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/)  
*piRandha* – divined her incarnation  
*periyAzhvAr* – (as) [periyAzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html)’s  
*peN piLLaiyAy* – divine daughter;  
*manamE, uNarndhu pAr* – Oh mind! Analyze and become clear about this.  
*uNdAgil* – if there is  
*oppu* – an equal  
*ANdALukku* – for [sUdik koduththa nAchchiyAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/), (then)  
*uNdu* – there would be an equal day  
*itharkum* – for this day too.

**vyAkyAnam**

Now as in this way she incarnated, he ([maNavALa mAmunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/)) divines the greatness of this star day through showing that there is no equal to this day, with ‘*periyAzhvAr peN piLLaiyAy’.*

It is the greatness of him ([AzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html)) who is the reason for [her](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) birth.

[periyAzhwAr](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/periyazhwar/) who was having the main kainkaryam of making flower garlands, found her ([ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/)) under a thiruththuzhAy (thuLasi) plant, and took her and gave her in the hands of his dhEviyAr (wife), and she was childless, she took this girl of someone else with a lot of affection; at that time, her breasts produced a lot of milk as said in ‘*van mulaigaL sOrndhu pAya* \[periyAzhvAr thirumozhi 2.2.4\]’ ((as yaSOdha saying to krishNan) my beautiful breasts due to my love for you are producing milk which is pouring out). It is usually only for the mothers who delivered their babies that the milk would produce when they have their children. So it talks about her being their girl child.

*[![periyazhwar-andal](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/periyazhwar-andal.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/periyazhwar-andal.jpg)periyAzhvAr’s divine daughter ANdAL*

***ANdAL piRandha*** – This is one (great) birth of [her](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) having greatness. nAchchiyAr who has got the worlds {bhUdhEvi} has come and incarnated in this world.

***piRandha –* ‘***nAnum piRandhamai poy anRE* \[nAchchiyAr thirumozhi – 10.4\]’ ((if chakkaravarththith thirumagan’s words are not truthful, then only) my birth would also be not truthful), is how she has talked (about [her](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) own birth).

The one who got (janakan) and the one who was born (sIthA pirAtti) also say ‘*mE pithA, me suthA* \[SrI rAmAyaNam – bhAla kAndam 73.27\]’ (my father, my daughter); in the same way [periyAzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html) too said ‘*oru magaL thannai udaiyEn* \[periyAzhvAr thirumozhi – 3.8.4\]’ (am having only one daughter (though this is said in a different context)), and his daughter [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) too said ‘*pattar pirAn kOthai*’ ([periyAzhvAr](http://ponnadi.blogspot.in/2012/10/archavathara-anubhavam-periyazhwar.html)’s daughter [gOdhA (AndAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/)).

***piRandha thiruvAdippUraththin seermai*** – Because of birth only the greatness is present for this day. When said there isn’t any other day that are mentioned till now that has got this greatness, then if there is a question as to why not, then,

***manamE uNarndhu pAr* –** Oh the mind which has got the ability to analyse well, instead of giving a cursory look, you think deeply again and again about this day.

**ANdALukku uNdAgil oppu itharkum uNdu –** It there is an equal to [ANdAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/) who rules all the worlds, then there would be an equal to this day also.

About her it was said ‘*avargaLilum kAttil vinjina kshamA dhayAdhi guNangaLaiyum nAvAl thogaikka oNNAdha azhagaiyum udaiyaLAy*’ (~ having qualities such as forgiving and kind mind compared to those others, and having beauty that cannot be expressed in words) is how it is divined.

So the meaning is that like how there is no equal to [her](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/12/16/andal/), there is no equal to her incarnation month and star day as well.
