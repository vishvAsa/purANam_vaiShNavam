## श्री देवराज अष्टकम् – श्लोक
श्री-  
श्रीमते शठकोपाये नम-  
श्रीमते रामानुजाये नम-  
श्रीमद्वरवरमुनये नम-

॥  ।
।----।----।
। [![thirukkachi-nambi-kanchi](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/thirukkachi-nambi-kanchi.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/thirukkachi-nambi-kanchi.jpg) । [![varadhar-3](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/varadhar-3.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/varadhar-3.jpg) ।

श्लोक 1

*नमस्ते हस्त्भी शैलेष ! श्रीमन ! अम्भ्जलोचन !* ।  
*शरणं त्वां प्रपन्नोस्मी प्रणतार्ति हराच्युत !* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-1)

हे हस्तगिरी नाथ! श्रीपति! अरविंदाक्ष! आपके चरणों में प्रणाम है। आप उनके दुखों का नाश करते है, जो आपके चरणों की आराधना करते है। आप अच्युत है, अपने दासों की सदा रक्षा करने वाले। मैं आपके चरणों में आश्रय लेता हूँ।

श्लोक 2

*समस्त प्राणी संत्राण प्रवीणकरुणोलबण* ।  
*विलसंतु कटाक्षास्ते मय्यस्मिन जगतांपते* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-2)

आप सभीजन की रक्षा करने में पुर्णतः सक्षम, दया/ कृपा से परिपूर्ण, इस जगत के एक मात्र रक्षक है। आपकी कृपामय दृष्टि मुझ पर भी अनुग्रहित हो।

श्लोक 3

*निन्दिताचार करणं निवृत्तम कृत्यकर्मण-* ।  
*पापियांसं अमर्यादम् पाहिमाम् वरद प्रभो!* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-3)

हे देवराज! उत्तम मनुष्यों द्वारा निषेध कार्यों को करनेवाला, अपने विहित कर्तव्यों को न करनेवाला, अपराधो में लिप्त और अनुशासनहीन मेरी, कृपया आप रक्षा करें।

श्लोक 4

*संसार मरुकांतारे ध्रुव्याधि व्याग्र भीषणे* ।  
*विषय क्षुद्र गुलमाद्ये तृषादपशालिनी* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-4)

श्लोक 5

*पुत्र धार गृह क्षेत्र मृग तृषण्म्बू पुष्कले* ।  
*कृत्या कृत्य विवेकान्दम परिभ्रान्तंइतस तथ-* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-5)

श्लोक 6

*अजश्रम जात तृष्णार्थ अवसंनांगमक्षमम्* ।  
*विलसंतु कटाक्षास्ते मय्यस्मिन जगतांपते* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-6)

श्लोक 7

*संतप्तं विविदैर दु-खै- ध्रुवचैरेवमाधीभि* ।  
*देवराज ! दयासिंधो देव देव जगतपते* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-7)

श्लोक 8

*त्वाधिक्षण सुधासिंधु विचि विक्षेपसिकरै* ।  
*कारुण्य मरूतानितै शीतलैरभीशिन्चमाम्* ॥

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/dhevarajashtakam-8)

इन पांच श्लोकों में वे अपनी स्थिति को उपमा के द्वारा दर्शाते है और देवराज भगवान से अपनी रक्षा की विनती करते है। यह जीवन एक मरुस्थल है। इस मरुस्थल में रोग भयावह व्याघ्र के समान है। यहाँ, तुच्छ सुख आदि काँटों के समान है। वासनाएं, पेड़ के समान है। पत्नी, संतान, घर, भूमि, संपत्ति आदि इस मरुस्थल में मृगजल के समान है। इस मरुस्थल में यह जाने बिना ही कि क्या करना चाहिए और क्या नहीं, मैं एक विक्षिप्त मनुष्य के समान भटक रहा हूँ, वह करते हुए जो नहीं करना चाहिए और उसे न करते हुए जिसे करना चाहिए, शरीर से दुर्बल, निर्बल/ क्षीण, मंद बुद्धि, सभी कार्यों में अयोग्य, आपके प्रति भक्ति से हीन, हे देवराज! दयासागर! देवो के देव! आप अपनी दया की शीतल पवन के द्वारा मेरी रक्षा करें, जो आपके आनंदायी रूप और आपकी प्रभूत कृपा की तरंगों से जल की बूंदों के समान बरसती है। मैं याचना करता हूँ कि आपकी यह कृपामयी दयादृष्टि मुझ पर भी हो।

-अदियेन भगवती रामानुजदासी

**pAsuram 38**

*emperumAnAr tharisanam enRE idharku*  
*namperumAL pErittu nAtti vaiththAr – ampuviyOr*  
*indhath tharisanaththai emperumAnAr vaLarththa*  
*andhach cheyal aRigaikkA.*
38

#### [Listen](http://yourlisten.com/sarathy.thothathri/upadhesaraththinamalai-38)

**Word by word meaning**

*ampuviyOr* – For the residents of earth  
*aRigaikkA* – to know (about),  
*andhach cheyal* – that deed/achievement of  
*emperumAnAr* – [SrI bhAshyakArar](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)  
*vaLarththa* – enriching/growing  
*indhath tharisanaththai –* this SrIvaishNava sampradhAyam,  
*namperumAL* – [emperumAn](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) of thiruvarangam  
*nAtti vaiththAr* – established firmly  
*pEr ittu* – (with) the divine name  
*idhaRku* – for this SrIvaishNava sampradhAyam  
*emperumAnAr tharisanam enRE* – as emperumAnAr dharisanam.

**vyAkyAnam**

He ([maNavALa mAmunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/)) is divining about [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)’s nurturing of the prapaththi mArgam (way of prapaththi), and divining SrI bAshyam and other granthams to protect that way, and nurturing this sampradhAyam based on that, and for every one to know this [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) naming this sampradhAyam based on [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)’s name, and establishing that firmly – by starting this pAsuram with ‘*emperumAnAr tharisanam’*.

While [SrIman nAthamunigaL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/22/nathamunigal/) and [yAmuna munigaL (ALavandhAr)](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/01/alavandhar/) are enriching the dharSanam, [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) pointed out its name as ‘*emperumAnAr dharSanam*’ based on [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)’s goal as only helping the others.

[![namperumal-nachiar_serthi2](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/namperumal-nachiar_serthi2-1024x768.jpg)](https://divyaprabandham.koyil.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/namperumal-nachiar_serthi2.jpg)*namperumAL in sErththi (together with thAyAr, a special once-in-a-year treat)*

It is similar to what is said in ‘*maryAdhAnAmcha lOkasya karththA kArayithA cha sa:* \[SrI rAmAyaNam sundhara kANdam 35.11\]’ (~SrI rAma is the doer of the laws of justice, and is one who makes them follow it).

That is, the noble meanings he got from [thirukkOttiyUr nambi](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/02/27/thirukkoshtiyur-nambi/) after walking several times, he donated that in a big way even at the expense of himself, and returned (to SrIrangam); then [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) who thought ‘*namakkAvArai nAmE thEdik koLvOm* (\~~we shall find the one who would be suitable for us), and so [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) gave very special name for this based on [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/), and said, from today ‘identify this path as emperumAnAr dharSanam’ (*‘emperumAnAr dharSanam ennungOL’),* and so divined his establishing of this, in this way in the world.

Or, instead of ‘*nAtti vaiththAr*’, when it is recited taught as ‘*nAttuviththAr*’ (made someone establish this), then, when [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) broke the command, crossed the boundary and taught prapaththi to others, seeing that greatness, [thirukkOttiyUr nambi](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/02/27/thirukkoshtiyur-nambi/) too in the same way gave him the divine name as ‘[emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)’, and said, ‘from today say this as emperumAnAr dharSanam \[guruparamparA prabhavam – page 195\]’, and was nurturing it, so [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) who divined to [(thirukkOttiyUr) nambi](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/02/27/thirukkoshtiyur-nambi/) to let this name be known, He Himself divined this through [nambi](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2013/02/27/thirukkoshtiyur-nambi/).

He ([emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/)) removed the loneliness of [emperumAn](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) that is said in ‘*ALumALAr* \[thiruvAimozhi 8.3.3\]’ (he is not having any one (and he himself is holding his conch and discus, nobody is involved in his divine feet, and so on)), and for removing the loneliness of the samsAris, by doing such teaching.

If asked what is the reason for [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) to establish this about dharSanam, he ([maNavALa mAmunigaL](http://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/mamunigal/)) says,

***‘ampuviyOr indhath tharisanaththai emperumAnAr vaLarththa andhach cheyal aRigaikkA’.***

Due to inclusion of the name [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) in it, the dharisanam which [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/) nurtured, the dharisanam which is of esteemed vaidhIkam, and is without any blemishes, which he grew through his teachings (upadhESam) – ([namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) established this name for the dharisanam) for the people of the beautiful world to know this deed of [emperumAnAr](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/09/06/emperumanar/).

***andhach cheyalai aRigiakkA(ga)*** – That is, for the people of the world to know such deed, and reach the true goal. That is, for the people of the world to know and reach true goal, he performed these deeds *as a help for the world* – [namperumAL](https://acharyas.koyil.org/index.php/2012/08/17/divya-dhampathi/) wanted the people to know this so he named it as *emperumAnAr dharisanam*.

By this, his growing of the the dharsanam is through the prapaththi sAsthram is the fact that is well established.
